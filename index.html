BERT Overview

BERT is a pre-trained deep learning model by Google for NLP tasks. It utilizes a Transformer architecture to capture word dependencies in sentences.


Context Understanding

BBERT is pre-trained on a large text corpus, enabling it to understand language nuances and context. Traditional rule-based approaches struggle with context-dependent errors, while BERT excels in understanding sentence context.


Bidirectional Nature

BERT considers both left and right contexts of words, crucial for understanding grammar in sentences.


Fine-Tuning for Grammar

Fine-tuning BERT on annotated grammar error datasets teaches it to recognize and correct various types of grammatical mistakes.


Semantic Understanding

BERT's ability to capture semantic and syntactic information allows it to understand sentence meaning and suggest corrections accurately.
